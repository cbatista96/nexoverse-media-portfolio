What's Included

Design, development and marketing in one package.

A beautiful store that nobody finds or that leaks customers at checkout is not a business. We build stores that look great, rank on Google, convert visitors and recover lost sales automatically.

Store Design & UX

High-converting store design built around the customer journey: clear product pages, frictionless cart flow, mobile-first layouts and trust signals that turn browsers into buyers.

Platform Setup (Shopify / WooCommerce)

Full platform configuration on Shopify or WooCommerce: product catalog, variants, collections, inventory management, shipping rules and tax settings — ready to sell from launch day.

Payment Gateway Integration

Secure payment setup with Stripe, PayPal, Apple Pay and local payment methods. Multi-currency support, SSL security and PCI-compliant checkout that customers trust and abandon less.

Email Automation & Recovery

Abandoned cart sequences, post-purchase flows, welcome series and re-engagement campaigns built in Klaviyo or Mailchimp — automated revenue that runs while you sleep.

On-Page SEO & Speed

Product and collection pages optimized for search: structured data, meta tags, image compression, Core Web Vitals tuning and schema markup so Google can find and rank your catalog.

Analytics & Conversion Tracking

Google Analytics 4, Google Tag Manager, Meta Pixel and enhanced e-commerce tracking configured so you always know where your revenue comes from and where customers drop off.

How It Works

Store live in 4 phases.

Phase 01

Discovery & Strategy

We map your product catalog, target customer, competitors and business goals. We choose the right platform, define the site architecture and agree on the full project scope before writing a single line of code.

Phase 02

Design & Content

Wireframes and high-fidelity mockups of key pages — homepage, product page, cart, checkout. You approve the design direction before development starts, avoiding costly revisions later.

Phase 03

Build & Integrate

Development on your chosen platform: theme customization or custom build, product catalog import, payment and shipping configuration, email flows, SEO setup and pixel/analytics integration.

Phase 04

Test, Launch & Handover

Full pre-launch QA on mobile and desktop, test order flow, speed audit. Launch day support plus a 30-day post-launch window for fixes. You get full access and training to manage your store independently.
